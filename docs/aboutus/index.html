Who is your Pharmacist?

My name is Printu, co-owner and Pharmacist in charge here at Medical Arts Pharmacy.

I’ve been a registered pharmacist since 2006, graduated from UCLA and obtained my PharmD from Western University of Health Sciences. I had worked for a large pharmacy chain for over 13 years and managed over 15 locations and 165 pharmacy employees before I decided to open up my own pharmacy. I want to use my expertise to bring a more personalized experience for my patients and do what I am most passionate about- improving patient quality of life and health outcomes. When you come in, you will likely work with me. If I am assisting patients, my support staff including Sandra, Judy, Nayomi and Araceli are here to assist you and answer your questions. Just ask for us by name.

The Independent Alternative

Millions of people every year endure the impersonal world of “big chain” pharmacies- standing in endless lines, waiting days for refills, rarely seeing the same pharmacist twice- and they generally do it for two reasons: They believe they are saving money. They don't realize they have a choice. At Medical Arts Pharmacy, we believe there is a better way. But before we tell you, why not read for yourself how other consumers just like you feel about independent drug stores?
